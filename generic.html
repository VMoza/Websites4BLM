How to be an Ally:

Recognize your privilege, whatever race you are and whatever hardships you’ve faced — you haven’t experienced the severe multifaceted discrimination as black people and never will. Recognize that a lot of our freedoms and rights are credited to movements and strives made by African-American activists during the civil rights era (Immigration act of 1965). They bore the burden for us so that our families could be here; it’s time for us to stand and bear the burden with them. Recognize that our privilege can be used to leverage support for movements. Support black owned businesses.
